# Citation Standards

Version: 1.0.0
Last updated: 2026-06-15

## Citation Rules

Include links for material factual claims that come from external sources.

For current-state facts, include the source date or retrieval date when the source is likely to change.

Use short quotes only when exact wording matters. Prefer paraphrase for normal findings.

## Evidence Table

For multi-source research, use:

```markdown
| Source | Type | Date | Relevant Finding | Confidence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
```

## Conflict Handling

When sources disagree:

- state the disagreement directly
- identify which source is more authoritative and why
- name what evidence would resolve it
- avoid smoothing over incompatible claims

## Citation Coverage Check

Before finalizing, scan the answer for:

- unsupported numbers
- unsupported "latest" or "current" claims
- product behavior claims without docs or tests
- benchmark claims without dataset, sample, scoring, and model details
- recommendations that imply current pricing, availability, or policy
